Acerca de mi

I'm a physics student at the Physics School of Universidad Veracruzana, in México, interested in complex networks, data science and computer competitions along those topics. Winner of the 2015-2016 “Festival de la Juventud" in the category “Networking and Computers”, he has published work in the scientific divulging journal “La ciencia y el Hombre” and was member of the winning team of the “Data Challenge Industrial 4.0” organized jointly by the CIIA's UV and LANIA A.C. He holds a Microsoft tech “Networking: Fundamentals” certification and a CompTIA “IT: Fundamentals” one. Currently he is involved in studying complex systems, artificial intelligence and scientific divulgation from a data analysis standpoint as a research assistant at the Research Center for Artificial Intelligence of the Universidad Veracruzana. He can be reached at eisaacjc8@gmail.com.
